IhamimAuto (“we”, “our”, or “us”) operates a multi-vendor e-commerce mobile application (the “App”). 1.1 Personal Information

When you register or use our App, we may collect:

Full name

Phone number

Email address

Delivery address

Profile information

Payment-related information (processed securely via third-party payment providers)

1.2 Vendor Information

If you register as a seller/vendor, we may collect:

Business name

Contact details

Product listings and pricing

Transaction and sales data

1.3 Automatically Collected Information

We may automatically collect:

Device information (model, OS version)

App usage data

IP address

Log data and crash reports

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

Create and manage user and vendor accounts

Process orders, payments, and deliveries

Enable communication between buyers and sellers

Improve app performance and user experience

Provide customer support

Send important notifications (order updates, security alerts)

Prevent fraud and unauthorized activities

Comply with legal obligations

3. Payments

Payments made through the App are processed using secure third-party payment gateways. We do not store or process sensitive payment information such as card numbers on our servers. Payment providers handle this information according to their own privacy policies.

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We may share your information only in the following cases:

With vendors: To allow sellers to fulfill orders (e.g., name, phone number, delivery address)

With service providers: Such as payment processors, hosting services, and analytics tools

For legal reasons: If required by law or to protect our rights, users, or public safety

With consent: When you explicitly approve information sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

6. User Rights

You have the right to:

You can manage most of your information directly within the App.

Our App is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we discover such data, we will delete it immediately.
